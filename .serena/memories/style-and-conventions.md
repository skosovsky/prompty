# Style and Conventions

- **Comments in code:** Always in English
- **Assistant responses:** Russian
- **Go style:** Standard Go conventions, golangci-lint
- **Naming:** PascalCase for exported, camelCase for unexported
- **Commit format:** type(scope): subject (feat, fix, docs, style, refactor, test, chore)
- **Rules:** See .cursor/rules/ for detailed React, Go, and integration guidelines
